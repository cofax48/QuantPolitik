Trade Climate

In this area, a nation's receptiveness to trade is evalauted. The logic stemming from the "Capitalist Peace Theory", in that nations that trade more, have more at stake, and are thus less likely to go to war. Additionally, the larger the mor receptive a nation is to trade, the more likely they are going to trade with on another. Such an incrasd likelihood, additionally increases the number of areas where contact may flourish and the number of sectors which may become entangled. Greater contact, entanglement, and capital flows leads to more individuals having closer relations, and a greater imperative for each government to maintain ever closer ties. Therefore, the components under consideration are:
Foreign Direct Investment, Trading Across Borders, GDP Growth Rate, Fortune Global 500, Trade Freedom, Change in Trade Freedom from 2015, and Stock Market Capitalization in billions
