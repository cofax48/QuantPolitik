Treatment

If you, or someone you know, has experienced a significant loss of visual field due to brain trauma or stroke, I am conducting FREE clinical trials of a method to help restore lost vision.

Loction

Training takes place at the Lighthouse Guild for the Blind,(15 West 65 st), has IRB approval and uses a special computerized program.

Availability

Only a few slots are open. Participant must be available for training at least once per week (for several months). Each session is a minimum of 90 min sessions. (Preference given to those with greater availability, motivation and handicap).
