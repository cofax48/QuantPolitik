What a QuantPolitik Score Shows?

QuantPolitik is a dynamic score generated by 147 unique data points that measure hard and soft power, economic and cultural exchange, as well as diplomatic interaction.

What We Do At QuantPolitik?

At QuantPolitik, we draw on the resources of think-tanks, institutions, governments and the media to provide detailed analysis on the political risk, resultant from America's relationship with any and all nations.
