<p>This is a <b>list of current foreign ministers</b> of the 193 <a href="/wiki/United_Nations_member_states" class="mw-redirect" title="United Nations member states">United Nations member states</a>, <a href="/wiki/Holy_See" title="Holy See">Holy See</a> (<a href="/wiki/Vatican_City" title="Vatican City">Vatican City</a>) and the <a href="/wiki/State_of_Palestine" title="State of Palestine">State of Palestine</a>.</p>
<p>Foreign ministers of <a href="/wiki/List_of_unrecognized_countries" class="mw-redirect" title="List of unrecognized countries">unrecognised sovereign countries</a> are included in a separate table.</p>
